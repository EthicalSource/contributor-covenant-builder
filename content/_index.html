Contributor Covenant:
A Code of Conduct for Digital Communities

A healthy community centers the shared values and norms of its members. While every community is different, there is a set of fundamental values and norms that are essential for fostering belonging and inclusion.

Contributor Covenant is a code of conduct that you can adapt to express both the central foundation and the special norms and values that distinguish your own community.

Adopting Contributor Covenant helps makes your community’s values explicit, and signals your commitment to creating a welcoming and safe environment for everyone.
